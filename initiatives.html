Senior School Initiatives

The Wellness Program

At The Choice School, we believe in the overall holistic development of the child, recognizing that mental health and well-being are integral components. We are committed to maintaining good mental health through various initiatives. In addition to bi-monthly class-wise sessions, we frequently organize batch-wise wellness sessions. These sessions aim to provide students with the necessary tools and support to navigate their emotions, develop resilience, and cultivate a positive mindset. Through these efforts, we strive to create a nurturing environment where every child feels valued, supported, and empowered to thrive academically, emotionally, and socially.

Awards Night

At The Choice School, our annual awards night is a celebration of our students' exceptional talents and achievements. From academic excellence to artistic brilliance, athletic prowess to leadership skills, the event showcases the diverse accomplishments of our vibrant community. It's a testament to dedication, perseverance, and growth, fostering a spirit of camaraderie and admiration. Beyond trophies, it's a celebration of character, resilience, and the unwavering pursuit of dreams.

Remedials

At The Choice School, we recognize that every student learns at their own pace. That's why we offer regular after-school remedial sessions where students can engage in personalized one-on-one or small group learning experiences. These sessions are designed to enhance understanding and skills, ensuring that each student receives the support they need to succeed academically. Our goal is to create a nurturing environment where every student can thrive and reach their full potential.

The Green Team

At The Choice School, our commitment to environmental sustainability has led us to establish a Green Team. This dedicated group of students and staff is actively engaged in initiatives aimed at promoting a cleaner and greener environment. Through multiple clean-up drives and a variety of other projects, we're not only improving our school's surroundings but also fostering a culture of environmental responsibility among our students and the wider community.
